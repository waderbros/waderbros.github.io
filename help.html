Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I use it with Apple Watch only?
A. Yes. SportRing works independently on Apple Watch. You can check your progress and change your goals, units, or ring color directly from your Watch.

Q. Does it work with iPhone and Apple Watch?
A. Yes. Your data and settings sync automatically. Any changes made on one device are instantly reflected on the other.

Q. How is Walk + Run distance calculated?
A. Walk + Run distance is the total estimated distance of walking and running detected throughout the day by your iPhone and Apple Watch. It is tracked separately from individual workouts.

Q. Where is my data stored?
A. All your data is stored safely on your device through Apple’s HealthKit. No information is sent to external servers or shared with third parties.

Q. How to change the app language?
The app and widget language follow your iPhone’s language settings. You can change the display language using the steps below.

  1. Open iPhone Settings
    Go to Settings → SportRing → Preferred Language.
  2. Select your preferred language
    Choose English, Japanese, or the language you want to use.
  3. If you also want the widget language to update
    If the widget does not switch languages, restarting your Apple Watch may apply the change.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

Widget language not changing

  1. Check iPhone system language
    Settings → General → Language & Region → iPhone Language. Make sure the language you want is selected.
  2. Check SportRing app-specific language
    Settings → SportRing → Preferred Language. Ensure the desired language is selected.
  3. Restart both devices
    Restart your iPhone and Apple Watch after changing language settings to ensure the widget reloads correctly.

Data or settings are not syncing

  1. Re-enter your settings and tap “Save” again (e.g., goal distance, unit, ring color).
  2. Open SportRing on both your iPhone and Apple Watch and keep them in the foreground for a few seconds.
  3. Ensure Bluetooth and Wi-Fi are enabled, and Airplane Mode is off.
  4. Check Health permissions:
    • iPhone Settings: Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → SportRing
    • or Health app: Data Access & Devices → SportRing
    • Make sure all workout-related categories are enabled.
  5. If it still doesn’t sync, restart both your Apple Watch and iPhone, then try again.

Supported Versions

  • Apple Watch: watchOS 10.6 or later
  • iPhone: iOS 17.6 or later
  • iPad: iPadOS 17.6 or later
